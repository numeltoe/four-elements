If you would like to create a shrine in order to pay homage to the element of Earth and encourage its energy's presence in your life, gather items that remind you of Earth. Follow your intuition in finding these items. They could be a handful of clay, brown cloths, or anything else that reminds you of the nurturing spirit, love, and acceptance of Earth. Find a sacred spot (perhaps one outside), and arrange the items there in a way that feels inspirational to you (feel free to experiment and rearrange them--there's no wrong answer! You can even shape the ground itself to make an Earth shrine), and take a moment now and then to come to the shrine and nurture your connection with the spirit of Earth whenever you feel the need to remind yourself of the comfort and nurturing love offered by the Earth.

Hügelkultur, which translates to "hill culture" in German, is a type of raised garden bed popular in permaculture gardening. A hügelkultur is made of mounded plant debris, such as branches and compost, covered with soil. Over time the buried material starts to break down, creating a rich medium for growing plants. You can create a hügelkultur yourself, which will no doubt strengthen your connection to the Earth! Learn how here.
